# 📊 Key Insights from Power BI Visualization
The exploratory analysis using Power BI revealed important patterns across payment methods, customer acquisition channels, product demand, and order status distribution.

# 1. 💳 Revenue by Payment Method
* Credit Card generated the highest total revenue
* Debit Card recorded the lowest contribution
# Insight:
* Customers prefer credit cards for higher-value transactions, making it the dominant payment channel for revenue generation.

# 2. 📣 Revenue by Referral Source
* Instagram contributed the highest total revenue
* Direct Referral generated the lowest revenue
# Insight:
* Social media (especially Instagram) is the most effective customer acquisition channel, while referral-based traffic is underperforming.

# 3. 📦 Quantity Sold by Product
* Chairs recorded the highest quantity sold
* Phones recorded the lowest quantity sold
# Insight:
* Furniture products (chairs) show stronger demand compared to electronics in this dataset, indicating product preference trends.

# 4. 📊 Orders by Status
* Canceled: 250 (highest)
* Returned: 247
* Pending: 237
* Shipped: 235
* Delivered: 231 (lowest)
# Insight:
*A significant proportion of orders are not reaching completion (high cancellations and returns), indicating potential issues in fulfillment, customer satisfaction, or product expectations.


# 📌 Overall Business Summary
* The analysis highlights that:
Credit cards dominate as the preferred payment method
Instagram is the strongest marketing channel
Certain products (chairs) outperform others significantly
Order completion rate is relatively low due to high cancellations and returns
* These insights can support:
Marketing optimization
Product strategy improvements
